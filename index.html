Do I have to necessarily register to shop on Doorvey?

You can surf the app without registration but only registered shoppers will be able to checkout and place orders. Registered members have to be logged in at the time of checking out the cart, they will be prompted to do so if they are not logged in.

Can I have multiple registrations?

Each email address and contact phone number can only be associated with one Doorvey account.

What are the modes of payment?

You can pay for your order on Doorvey using the following modes of payments. Cash on delivery, Credit and debit cards (VISA / Mastercard / Rupay), Mobile wallets(Paytm, Phonepe etc) and UPI.

Is it safe to use my credit/ debit card on Doorvey?

Yes it is absolutely safe to use your card on Doorvey app. A recent directive from RBI makes it mandatory to have an additional authentication pass code verified by VISA (VBV) or MSC (Master Secure Code) which has to be entered by online shoppers while paying online using visa or master credit card. It means extra security for customers, thus making online shopping safer. The payment gateway we use is the safest payment gateway.

What is Doorvey Wallet?

The Doorvey Wallet is a pre-paid credit account that is associated with your Doorvey account. This prepaid account allows you to pay a lump sum amount once to Doorvey and then shop multiple times without having to pay each time.

How will the delivery be done?

We have a dedicated team of delivery personnel and a fleet of vehicles operating across the city which ensures timely and accurate delivery to our customers.
